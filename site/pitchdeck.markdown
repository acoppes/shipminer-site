# Elevator Pitch 

Ship Miner is a relaxing miner roguelike game with unique retro  1-bit pixel art aesthetics and combat elements where you:

* Explore and Mine minerals from procedural generated asteroids.
* Upgrade your Ship with gameplay changer technologies.
* Combat different threats.

The game offers an retro immersive experience that exercises player's imagination.

Platforms 

* PC and Steam Deck through Steam
* Consoles (need help for this) after PC.

State

* Alpha Stage: still defining last loops of the game and needs lots of content. 
* Public Demo
* Private Playtest

Release

* Aiming to release EA in Q3 of 2026, and looking for funding and marketing services for the last stage of development.
